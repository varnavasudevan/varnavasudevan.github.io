Podo Labs
User Research, Interface Design, Industrial Design
During the Spring of 2016, I had the opportunity to work with Podo Labs as a Product Design Consultant with Berkeley Innovation.

I worked with Vy Luu, Eunice Kim, and Felix Yiu to provide insights into the physical and digital user experience of Podo, the world's first stick-and-shoot camera. Our team, mentored by Sophia Morgan, conducted user research, crafted personas, conducted usability tests on the current mobile application, and prototyped a wearable accessory to help users more easily carry the Podo camera on-the-go.
