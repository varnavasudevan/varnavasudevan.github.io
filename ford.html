Ford Motor Company
User Research, Interaction Design, Prototyping
During the summer of 2017, I had the opportunity to work with the Advanced UX team at the Ford Research and Innovation Center in Palo Alto, California. I worked on both physical and digital interaction design challenges, and spent 12 weeks conducting user research, creating journey maps, hosting ideation workshops, and prototyping concepts. The focus of my work was in the autonomous vehicle space, working on both near-term and moonshot programs, and some key snapshots of my work can be seen below. I am happy to discuss my work at Ford in more detail, so please feel free to reach out!
