About Me
Hello, I'm Varna!

I am a senior at UC Berkeley pursuing a degree in Mechanical Engineering, with a passion for human-centered design.
  • I am currently a Research Assistant and Project Manager at theDesignExchange, a collaborative research effort between Berkeley and MIT to create the largest repository of design methods in the world.

  • This past summer I was a UX Research intern at Lyft digging into motivations and painpoints for both drivers and passengers, and reimagining the future of transportation!

  • I was previously an Experience Design intern at the Ford Research and Innovation Center in Palo Alto with a focus on Physical and Digital Interaction Design to envision the future of autonomous vehicles.

  • I was formerly President of Berkeley Innovation, a student-run human-centered design consultancy on campus.
