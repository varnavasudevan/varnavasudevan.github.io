theDesignExchange
Design Researcher, Content Writer, Project Manager




theDesignExchange is an online interactive portal for designers and researchers. The content on this portal is currently being used for 6 graduate-level courses at Haas School of Business and Berkeley Engineering as a valuable resource when teaching human-centered design methodology.

Working alongside graduate students and professors at MIT, the Berkeley Institute for Design, and the Berkeley BEST Lab, I have conducted user research and interviewed design educators at the university level to get a detailed understanding of our user base as well as inform future iterations of the user interface. In addition, I have been creating and writing design methodology content for the platform, and will be managing a team of three undergraduate method writers in the upcoming spring semester.

I also had the opportunity to write a thesis paper that delves into the process of redesigning theDesignExchange method page to assist novice designers in embedding design methods into practice.
