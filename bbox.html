Breakfast Box
User Research, Industrial Design, Interface Design
During the Fall of 2016, I had the opportunity to work with Nourish Technologies as a Product Design Consultant with Berkeley Innovation.

I worked with Jess Liu, Marisa Ahmed, and Kate Lee to find painpoints and reimagine the customer experience for BreakfastBox (BBOX), an automated breakfast eatery that uses robots to prepare and serve fresh breakfast items. Our team, mentored by Tim Guan and Karen Li, conducted user research, synthesized insights, and prototyped both a physical pick-up interface as well as a mobile ordering application.
